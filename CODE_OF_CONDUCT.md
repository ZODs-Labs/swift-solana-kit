# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community welcoming and respectful for everyone, regardless of identity, background, experience or personal circumstance.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps this community:

- Use welcoming language.
- Respect different viewpoints.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for users and maintainers.
- Take responsibility for mistakes.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or intimidation.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Insults, personal attacks or political attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests and other contributions that do not meet these standards. Maintainers may also restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or community.

## Reporting

Report Code of Conduct concerns to sol.engineer020@gmail.com.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly. Maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
